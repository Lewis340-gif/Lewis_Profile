AI Capabilities Delivering Value Today

The most impactful AI applications today are those that augment human capabilities rather than replace them.

Key AI capabilities that are leading in measurable value

  • Enterprise Search: Empowers employees by improving access to internal knowledge and reducing time spent searching for information.
  • Content Generation and Summarization: Improves productivity for both clinicians and administrative staff.
  • Contact Center Agents: Reduces response times and improves customer satisfaction.
  • Office Productivity Tools: Accelerates content creation, editing, and collaboration capabilities.
  • Clinical Decision Support: Saves clinicians time by extracting key medical insights from large volumes of clinical data to support decision-making.
  • Project Management: Helps teams stay coordinated by AI reading and summarizing project updates and generating status reports on demand.
  • Coding Assistants: Aids developers by significantly reducing development time and improving code quality.
  • Reporting & Analytics: Enables non-technical people to engage with data by asking natural language questions to get actionable insights.
  • Revenue Cycle Management: Reads payor responses and automatically generates appropriate draft responses and identifies probable follow-up actions.
  • Agentic AI: Performs routine tasks, such as scheduling appointments or managing workflows, reducing the need for constant human oversight.

Without clean, curated, and accessible data, even the most advanced AI tools will fail to deliver value.

Product Management & UX Design plays a Critical Role

The single biggest competitive advantage will be to identify and understand deep-rooted human problems first, then apply the appropriate technology to help solve the problem.


Application development is democratizing: non-technical teams are now building applications and taking lead on design and testing, IT's role will evolve to strategic consultancy while maintaining responsibility for execution.
