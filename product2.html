Integration of Product Management & UX Design

Incorporating a strong product management approach and user-centered design practice will help to ensure successful deployment and adoption within healthcare environments.

Why Product Management & UX Design Matter

Estimates regarding artificial intelligence (AI) project failures vary, with some studies indicating that 80% to 95% of AI initiatives fail to deliver expected, measurable returns.

KEY REASONS FOR AI FAILURES:

  • Lack of Strategic Alignment: Organizations often focus on the tool rather than the specific, high-value problem.
  • Failure to Move Past Pilot: 70-90% of AI projects do not scale beyond the initial pilot phase because of a failure to integrate the AI solution into existing workflows and organizational structures.

  • Organizational and Cultural Barriers: Many companies lack the necessary data infrastructure, skills, and organizational readiness for AI implementation.

Product management and UX design practices directly address the first two bullet points from the outset of AI projects.
